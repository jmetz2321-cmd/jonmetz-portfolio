## Deployment

Deploy as a static site to any of:
- **Vercel** — `vercel deploy --prod` (or use `/deploy` skill)
- **Netlify** — drag-and-drop the `portfolio/` folder at netlify.com/drop
- **GitHub Pages** — push to a repo, enable Pages in settings

For a custom domain (e.g. `jonmetz.com`), connect it through your hosting provider's dashboard after deploying.
